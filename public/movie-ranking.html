The Best Movies According To Bruke

My top three movies all-Time.

Hacksaw Ridge

A beautiful story of an idealist. During the a time of war when young men were being sent to fight the japanese, a fellow patriot decides to join his brothers in arms to fight but makes a contreversial decision to not use firearms.

Princes of Egypt

A beautiful animated story about Exodus. Using old style animation and drawing depicted beautifully. A masterpiece.

Half GirlFriend

A beautiful story about love and perserverance for people he cares and about how far he is willing to go for it.
